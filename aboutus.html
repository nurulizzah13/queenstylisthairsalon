About Us

Queen Stylist Hair Saloon is business that operates in a small industry which involved in the salon industry. Our company chose Queen Stylist Hair Salon as the name of our company because to symbolize that our salon is specially designed for women especially Muslim women who love privacy to cover their ‘aurah’. Our company is specially designed to achieve our main objective which is to provide the best experience for women at hair salon. We believe that our concern towards customer privacy will attract more women to come to our salon to experience the best services that we provide.
